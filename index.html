My Coffee Story

My name is Philemon Tamang, a dedicated coffee professional from Nepal with over three years of hands-on barista experience in Dubai. Throughout my journey, I have developed expertise in espresso extraction, latte art, customer service, and specialty coffee preparation.

Growing up in Nepal, surrounded by beautiful hills where coffee is cultivated, inspired my passion for coffee culture and quality. I believe every cup represents craftsmanship, dedication, and a unique story. My mission is to create memorable coffee experiences while continuously expanding my knowledge of coffee roasting, brewing techniques, and specialty coffee innovation.
